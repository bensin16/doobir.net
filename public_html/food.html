cheap ass beef and broccoli

makes like 4 servings

source

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 12 ounces of brocc florets
  • a couple green onions

Instructions

  1. make 1 cup of brown rice (takes like 50 minutes)
  2. in small mixing bowl, whisk soy sauce, rice vinegar, minced garlic, brown sugar, ginger, sesame oil and sriracha
  3. heat olive oil in big skillet on medium high heat. Add beef and brown for like 3-5 minutes. break it apart and drain fat
  4. Add in and combine broccoli, green onions, rice and sauce and simmer for 3-4 minutes
  5. plate and serve
